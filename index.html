Product Features

Sleep Detection

The Eye Blink Sensors are installed in the car to check if the person is felling drowsy/sleepy and ring the alarm in the car so that the person wakes up.

Accident Detection and Driver Safety

Vibration sensor in the Vehicle detects the accident and sends the location of the accident to an authorized person via GSM.

Pedestrian and Vehicle Safety

The Proximity Sensors installed in the car detects if there is any pedestrian or vehicle comes too close to the vehicle and then stops the vehicle from accident.

Alcohol Detection

The Gas MQ-4 Sensor detects if the driver is drunk and stops the engine from being ignited if the person is under influence of Alcohol.

Seat Belt Check Mechanism

Infrared Sensors are installed in the seatbelt to check if the person is wearing seatbelt or not and is driving safely.

How it works?

In this modern era, transportation is one of the important need of human. We face lot of problems in it which cost human life. This system deals with problem which cause accident and also to ensure safety and security of the person driving the vehicle. It deals with vibration sensor to detect the accident. If the vibration sensor detects the accident, an alert message is sent to the official person with the GPS location in form of latitude and longitude. A mechanism involves confirming the assurance of locked seat belt. It also ensures the driver is not drunk. Only if driver is not get drunk, then only the ignition will start. The eye blink sensor detects the drowsiness of the driver and give alert using the buzzer. It also deploys a proximity sensor in order to avoid the collision. The automotive proximity sensor are deployed inside the car to find out the interruption in path. All these status will be updated on the internet and the same is also be deployed on an app. Through this, driver safety is ensured through an automotive mechanism.
